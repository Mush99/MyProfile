Project

Overview


My project is a console game, which is heavily influenced by the mobile game “Flappy Bird”. Console gamers never had the chance to experience the
worldwide hit game “Flappy bird” for themselves, so I want to give them that experience. The game won’t be an exact copy of the mobile game, because
consoles can handle more graphics and performance, the game will be 3D and have more elements implemented to it, such as power ups, new skins,
and more obstacles to bypass. Similarly, to “Flappy Bird” there is no end to the game and will be infinite, so users around the world can try
beating each other's score, as scores will be put online for people to compete.


Motivation


The reason I want to create a game like “Flappy Bird” is because I remember how fun it was when it first came out, the countless hours I
spent playing the game and it links me to memories of middle school. So, I want to relive those moments but want to add a twist to it and enjoy
it with better graphics and attributes and bringing it to the console is the best way. Another reason I want to remake “Flappy Bird”, is so
kids of this generation can also experience what we did and show them how challenging games can be. Finally, it is a way for gamers to pay for a
game and be able to enjoy it for a long time as every time you lose you know you can start again and try beating your high score or the rest of the worlds.


Description


“Rappy Bird” will be a single player game, that will be available for consoles such as the Xbox, PS4, and Nintendo Switch. Like many games
it will consist of an online leader board, which will be broken in too few categories. The categories will
be; Local, which compares you to the people on your friends list, Nation. which compares you to the people that are currently living in the same country as you, World Wide.
Which compares you to the whole globe. Being in the top ten in the two categories of Nation and World Wide will reward players with daily in-game currency so they can spend it at the shop to buy
skins, etc. Continuing from the in-game currency, depending on how far you can get on each run players are rewarded with Rappies which the in-game currency is,
and can be used at the shop. The currency will not be purchasable with money unlike most games and will have to be earned, so it gives more committed players the rewards they deserve.
The shop will mainly consist of skins for players to customise their birds with, skins will be a reasonable price and will have rarity to them depending on each
skin. Skins will all be purchasable by Rappies, and can’t be bought with real currency, so only players that are committed will have the satisfaction of
owning rare skins. Also, in the shop different sceneries can be bought, sceneries don’t add any boost or specialties and are just there for aesthetics. The main game will consist of pipes,
buildings, etc depending on the scenery that the player must try and avoid, the further they get the faster their character gets and higher and lower the obstacles
are. Other obstacles that must be avoided are other birds, clouds, rubbish thrown, etc.


Tools & Skills


The game will be running on Unreal Engine 4, as it will give the best graphics and performance for consoles to run. Also, open sources
will be used to get some of the classic “Flappy Bird” aesthetics to be used in the default world and character. Skills needed for
this will be me needing to have an advance amount of knowledge on Unreal Engine 4 and its language so I can add all the new aspects that I want to
implement, similarly I need to know how to know reach my target audience and what entertain them. As the game is pretty much a reboot, I need to engineer it so it can capture the eyes
of the old generation users and to capture the new generation of users. So, not only will I need skills for computers, but also,
I need social skills to capture my audience to enjoy my game.
